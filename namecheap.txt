1.buy one domain name
2.create a hosting zone in route 53
3.update the name servers in domain page.
(takes upto 48hrs)
4.create ssl in certificate manager service in aws.
5. add certificate in route 53.
6.keep your webpages in s3 bucket
7.create one cloudfront for the s3 bucket (with certificate)
8. create record for cloudfront in route 53
9.test your website.
